Survey Data Analysis

Introduction

This report demonstrates the analysis of survey data collected using the shinysurveys package. The goal is to explore the data and provide students with a practical introduction to creating dynamic reports using Quarto.

Questionnaire

The table below provides a codebook for the survey questionnaire, listing variable names and the corresponding questions:

Survey Questionnaire Codebook
variable_name question
marital What is your marital status?
age What is your age?
race What is your race?
rincome What is your income range?
partyid What is your political affiliation?
relig What is your religion?
tvhours How many hours per day do you spend watching TV?
